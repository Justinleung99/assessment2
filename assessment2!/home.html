This is Palace

Palace Skateboards, is a British skateboarding brand created by Lev Tanju in 2009. We are best known for the clothing line and skate team. Having collaborated over the years with sportswear brands such as Umbro, Reebok, and Adidas, Palace has grown to become a popular international streetwear brand, putting out collections every season that are available through our online and flagship stores. Palace is also known for the exclusivity in difficulty of procurement due to high demand and low supply, leading to comparisons between it and fellow streetwear brand Supreme in their mutual notoriety of low supply.
